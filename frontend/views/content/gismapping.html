GIS Mapping
  • G-Source creates highly detailed and intricate GIS maps from hard copies and raw data, so every inch of your space is accounted for.
  • Cutting edge GIS mapping technology in the form of LIDAR point cloud mapping, cadastral, utility, land cover and parcel mapping helps in streamlining and organizing land related data for analysis and decision making.
  • Capture, create and manage detailed GIS maps in line with international standards for use across a variety of verticals, including telecommunication, power management, water management, town planning, urban development, natural resource management and transportation networks.
GEO DATA CONVERSION
  • G-Source converts critical data from any format to GIS friendly formats through our extensive data capturing and conversion service. From large paper maps to image files and ortho-photos, you can now capture key data and create geodatabases for greater utility.
  • We also offer data conversion between different vector formats, data capture from both textural and vector resources and accurate geocoding.
  • We also handle the conversion of ortho-photos and differential rectification, for you to effectively source and organize data from high quality ortho-photos and drone images.
