About G-Source

G-Source connects the dots between data, design and delivery.

Started in 2014, G-Source offers a unique combination of end-to end customized engineering, design, GIS and technology solutions. In a short span, we have created a niche for ourselves becoming agents of change in the drafting, designing and engineering segments by adopting relevant and cutting edge technologies like GIS and Business Intelligence. Not only are we capable of converting raw data into industry standard designs and maps, we are also well equipped to consult our clients on developing full fledged applications on top of these processed data.

Based out of Florida, we have a reputation of delivering drafting, plotting , engineering design as well as mapping projects with much aplomb in several parts of the United States. We understand the surveyor's mindset as well as the industry expectations when it comes to accuracy of processed CAD data. This has helped us maintain extremely high standards of accuracy and our highly renewable client relationships stand testament to that.

Our business is built on the following key aspects which we consider as elements of our DNA:

  • Customer-centric approach.
  • No compromise on accuracy and efficiency.
  • ‘First time right’ delivery every time.
  • Determined resolve to meet deadlines.

    Sandeep Mahadik

    Managing Director

    A young and enterprising student bustling with ideas which were often tagged as outrageous and ahead of his times, Sandeep knew it during his MBA days that he was smitten by the entrepreneurial bug. Poised with an evangelist’s zeal to pursue his passion, he successfully launched and ran multiple businesses ranging from online contracting and sub-contracting of local service providers, online biding portals and recruiting. These were his building blocks and he learnt the ropes of running a business with a fair share of success and failure with much aplomb. His next adventure was with his confidant and friend Monojit - a claims adjustment & mortgage processing unit which they ran with considerable success. It is during these days that, after a lot of brainstorming, both Sandeep and Monojit stumbled upon this idea of working around land survey and signage drafting pandering to the surveyors & engineers in the US. This idea blossomed into what everyone knows as G-Source today which is a 50 people company and growing stronger by the day. Already working with clients across 20 states in the US, Sandeep is spearheading G-Source in the next phase into GIS, BIM & creating customized applications for our clients in the US and all around the world.
