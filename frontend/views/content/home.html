our services

cad drafting

With CAD, you can say goodbye to the tedious task of manual drafting, designs and design documentation. A digital draftsman with pin point accuracy, CAD is the answer to all your designing and visualisation needs. Be it stadiums, hospitals, pumping stations, oil refineries, manufacturing facilities, water purification plants, and railway tracks/stations, the use of CAD helps to visualise concepts through photorealistic renderings and simulate how a design will perform in the real world.

gis mapping

Geographic Information System (GIS) is the most sophisticated system to capture, store, manipulate, analyse, manage, and present spatial or geographic data. Right from plotting geospatial data like water and gas utilities to analysing flood and storm patterns to developing complex location based applications like cadastral mapping based taxation, 911 response services and maintaining water networks in cities to name a few, GIS is the way ahead to build technically superior decision making tools.

about g-source

Started in 2014, G-Source offers a unique combination of end-to end customized engineering, design, GIS and technology solutions. In a short span, we have created a niche for ourselves becoming agents of change in the drafting, designing and engineering segments by adopting relevant and cutting edge technologies like GIS and Business Intelligence. Not only are we capable of converting raw data into industry standard designs and maps, we are also well equipped to consult our clients on developing full fledged applications on top of these processed data.

Based out of Florida, we have a reputation of delivering drafting, plotting , engineering design as well as mapping projects with much aplomb in several parts of the United States. We understand the surveyor's mindset as well as the industry expectations when it comes to accuracy of processed CAD data. This has helped us maintain extremely high standards of accuracy and our highly renewable client relationships stand testament to that.
